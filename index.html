Meet Redeuce

ReDeuce™ is an innovative, eco-conscious tennis ball engineered to deliver professional-level performance while eliminating end-of-life waste. Designed with sustainability at its core, ReDeuce™ is made entirely from recyclable, non-toxic materials, offering athletes and recreational players a guilt-free way to enjoy the game they love.

In addition to reducing waste, ReDeuce™ promotes a closed-loop production model, where used balls can be returned, reprocessed, and remanufactured into new ones, offering clubs, schools, and leagues a sustainable alternative that does not compromise quality.

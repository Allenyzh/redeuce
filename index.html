Meet Redeuce

ReDeuce™ is an innovative, eco-conscious tennis ball engineered to deliver professional-level performance while eliminating end-of-life waste. Designed with sustainability at its core, ReDeuce™ is made entirely from recyclable, non-toxic materials, offering athletes and recreational players a guilt-free way to enjoy the game they love.

Traditional tennis balls are composed of synthetic rubber and mixed-material felt, which are difficult—if not impossible—to recycle. As a result, over 125 million tennis balls are discarded annually in North America alone, often ending up in landfills. ReDeuce™ reimagines the tennis ball using monomaterial or compatible recyclable components, such as thermoplastic elastomers (TPE) for the core and recycled PET-based felt for the outer layer. The result is a high-performance, pressurized ball that meets playing standards and can be fully processed through standard recycling streams.

In addition to reducing waste, ReDeuce™ promotes a closed-loop production model, where used balls can be returned, reprocessed, and remanufactured into new ones, offering clubs, schools, and leagues a sustainable alternative that does not compromise quality.

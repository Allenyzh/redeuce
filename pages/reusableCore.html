Reusable Core — The Heart of Sustainable Performance

June 12, 2025 • by ReDeuce Team

At ReDeuce™, we believe performance and sustainability should go hand in hand. Our innovative tennis ball core is engineered to last over 3x longer than standard balls, dramatically reducing waste without sacrificing bounce, control, or feel.

Traditional tennis balls degrade quickly — not only does that shorten their lifespan, but it also contributes to the 300 million tennis balls that end up in landfills every year. Our reusable core is designed with durable, recyclable materials that retain their shape and elasticity over time.

Engineered for Durability

Through rigorous testing with athletes and coaches, the ReDeuce core has shown exceptional resistance to pressure loss and cracking, even after hours of intense play. Whether on clay, grass, or hard court, the performance remains consistent.

Environmental Impact

Every ReDeuce ball prevents over 200g of non-biodegradable rubber from being discarded. Multiply that by hundreds of players, and the impact becomes massive. Our core technology supports a closed-loop model: old balls are returned, reprocessed, and re-manufactured — no waste, no compromise.

Join us in making tennis more sustainable — one bounce at a time.
